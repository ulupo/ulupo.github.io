Umberto Lupo

Senior AI Scientist at Absci, working on de novo design of antibody binders.

Lausanne, Switzerland

Previously a post-doctoral researcher at the <a href=https://www.epfl.ch/labs/bitbol-lab/>Bitbol Lab</a>, EPFL, Switzerland. There, I dabbled in protein language models and their applications to protein-protein interactions and multimer structure prediction, de novo protein sequence generation, and phylogenetics.

I have written and/or maintained several open-source software libraries in topological machine learning.

My PhD was on algebraic quantum field theory on curved spacetimes, which is a general and mathematically rigorous approach to quantization on arbitrary spacetimes.
